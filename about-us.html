Our Mission

NEGOTIATION ISN’T WAR. BOTH SIDES MUST WIN.

Recon Realties is a boutique real estate brokerage firm that puts the interests and concerns of our clients first. We provide corporate-quality service by relationship-driven trusted advisors. We deliver fully integrated, creative commercial real estate solutions with professional skill and integrity. Our focus includes office and industrial leasing, sales and investment properties. Our unique ability to understand and respond to the needs of owners, tenants and investors has enabled us to obtain industry recognition. Repeat business and client referrals attest to the continued professional quality of our representation. At Recon Realties, we are fully committed to our clients’ success.

Meet our team

Eric Purcell is the founder of Recon Realties. He has over twenty-five years of real estate experience in the Montreal marketplace. His focus is servicing small and medium-sized companies in the leasing, acquisition and disposition of industrial and office spaces. A long list of satisfied clients can attest that Eric applies the same diligent work ethic, honesty and results-oriented focus to the investment market, as a natural extension of his leasing work. With experience and expertise in negotiation and problem solving, he is able to secure the best deal for his clients. The latest technology and a wealth of contacts gathered over more than two decades allow Eric to combine the personal touch of a boutique firm with the wider reach of a multi-national company.
